## 📱 Setup (Do This First!)

### On Your Phone:
1. Open the link in your browser (Safari/Chrome)
2. Tap **Share** (📤) → **Add to Home Screen**
3. Now you have an app icon — tap it anytime!

### On Your Work Computer:
1. Open the link in your browser
2. Bookmark it or save to bookmarks bar

---

## 📸 Adding Expenses

### Option 1: Upload a Receipt Photo
1. Tap **Upload Receipt** (left sidebar)
2. Take a photo or select from gallery
3. The app auto-fills vendor, date, and amount
4. **Check & edit** the details if needed
5. Select the right **Category**
6. Tap **Save Expense**

### Option 2: Manual Entry
1. Tap **Upload Receipt**
2. Click **"Skip, enter manually"** (just leave the upload empty)
3. Fill in the details
4. Tap **Save Expense**

---

## 🏷️ Setting Up Your Categories

Go to **Settings** (left sidebar) to add categories that match your clinic:

**Suggested categories:**
- Laboratory & Diagnostics (keywords: blood test, lab, cbc, urinalysis)
- Treatments & Procedures (keywords: surgery, vaccination, consultation)
- Medical Supplies
- Pharmaceuticals
- Equipment & Maintenance
- Clinic Maintenance
- Utilities
- Office Supplies

**How keywords work:** When you upload a receipt, the app looks for these words and auto-suggests the category. Add words that appear on your common receipts.

---

## 📊 Viewing Your Expenses

- **Dashboard** — overview of spending, charts, recent expenses
- **Expenses** — full list with filters (date, category, search)

---

## 📋 Reports & Export

1. Go to **Reports**
2. Pick a report type and date range
3. Click **Generate**
4. To download: go to **Expenses** → **Export CSV**

**⚠️ Important:** Export your data regularly as a backup! Go to Expenses → Export CSV.

---

## ❓ Quick Tips

- **Edit an expense:** Expenses list → click the pencil icon ✏️
- **Delete an expense:** Expenses list → click the trash icon 🗑️
- **Search expenses:** Use the search box in Expenses
- **Filter by date:** Use the date pickers in Expenses
